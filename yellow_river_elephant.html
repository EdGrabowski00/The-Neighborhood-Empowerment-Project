The Neighborhood Empowerment Project

Welcome to The Neighborhood Empowerment Project!

We are an organization committed to empowering our local neighborhoods and communities through sustainable development and education initiatives. Our organization strives to cultivate an environment that fosters growth and opportunity for everyone. We believe that when people come together, meaningful change can happen!

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring communities together to build a brighter future. We strive to create a sustainable, safe, and vibrant environment — one in which families, businesses, and neighborhoods thrive. We are committed to listening to the needs of our communities and responding with effective action.

Our Projects

We have a multitude of projects that we work on both locally and nationally. Our projects range from neighborhood clean-ups to creating educational opportunities for underserved areas. We are always looking for volunteers, donations, and sponsors for these projects. Get involved today!
